Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common queries regarding shopping, shipping, and setup support.

Do you deliver outside Mumbai?

Yes! While we offer free same-day shipping within Mumbai limits, we also deliver major household electronic items to adjoining cities and other states across India through verified premium logistic partners. Shipping charges may apply based on dimensions and state regulations.

What kind of warranty is offered on appliances?

Every single item sold at Gada Electronics is brand new and comes with a 100% genuine brand warranty card. Standard appliance warranties range between 1 to 2 years, with extended compressor or panel warranties (up to 10 years) offered directly by manufacturers like Sony, Samsung, and LG. We also provide our own optional extended support warranty covers.

Is standard installation support free?

Yes, standard installation and product demonstration are completely free for all television models (wall-mounting brackets included), refrigerator placements, washing machines, and split air conditioner installations within 24 hours of delivery. Brand-authorized service engineers will perform the setup at your home.

What payment options and EMI finance schemes do you support?

We support Cash, Credit and Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), and Netbanking. Additionally, we provide Zero Down-payment & No-cost EMI financing options at our showroom through Bajaj Finserv, HDFC bank cards, and other major financial institutions.
